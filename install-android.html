How to Install Apollo TV on Android

Install Apollo TV on any Android phone or tablet in minutes for IPTV streaming.

Step 1 — Allow Unknown Sources

Settings → Security → enable Install unknown apps for your browser or file manager.

Step 2 — Download APK

Open browser and visit: https://apollotv-8k.com/apollo.apk (use your APK link). Download the file.

Step 3 — Install

Open the downloaded APK, grant permissions, and tap Install.

Step 4 — Launch & Login

Open Apollo TV, sign in with credentials, and enjoy live channels and on-demand content.
