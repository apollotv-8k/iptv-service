How to Install Apollo TV on Firestick

Follow these simple steps to install Apollo TV on your Amazon Firestick for fast IPTV streaming.

Step 1 — Enable Apps from Unknown Sources

Go to Settings → My Fire TV → Developer Options → turn on Apps from Unknown Sources.

Step 2 — Install Downloader

Open the Amazon Appstore, search for Downloader and install it.

Step 3 — Download Apollo TV

Open Downloader and enter: https://apollotv-8k.com/apollo.apk (replace with your APK path). Download and install.

Step 4 — Open and Enjoy

Launch Apollo TV, sign in, and stream live channels, movies, and sports.
